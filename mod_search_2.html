30 years of Mod Archive

Friday 6th February 2026


This month, The Mod Archive turns 30 years old. At that point in time, the oldest piece of tracked music was less than ten years old, and none of the original founders would probably have believed if you told them that their site would live on for that long!
We're very grateful to all the people that have kept it alive during this time - all former and present staff members, but also all the members that decided to share their music with the rest of the world, write a comment or extensive review to unearth the gems among the many, many modules you can now find on the website.
